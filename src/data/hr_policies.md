# Nrolled Company Policies

## 1. Leave Process
All employees are entitled to 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per calendar year.
To apply for leave, employees must submit a request through the Nrolled Leave Management portal at least 14 days in advance for vacations longer than 3 days.
For sick leave, employees must notify their manager before 9:00 AM on the day of absence. Sick leave is capped at 10 days per year.
Unused PTO can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.

## 2. Job Creation
To create a new job posting, a department head must submit a "Role Requisition Form" to the HR department.
The form must include:
- Job Title
- Department
- Expected Salary Range
- Key Responsibilities
- Required Qualifications
Once submitted, the Finance department must approve the budget before the job is officially posted on the careers page.

## 3. Payroll Basics
Payroll is processed on the 25th of every month. If the 25th falls on a weekend or public holiday, payroll will be processed on the preceding business day.
Employees are required to submit their timesheets by the 20th of the month.
Overtime must be pre-approved by a manager and is paid at 1.5x the hourly rate.
Bonuses are paid out in the February payroll cycle, based on the previous year's performance review.

## 4. Workforce Management
Nrolled follows a hybrid work model. Employees are expected to be in the office at least 3 days a week.
Core working hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's local time zone, during which all employees should be available for meetings.
Flexibility outside core hours is allowed, provided the 40-hour work week is met.
